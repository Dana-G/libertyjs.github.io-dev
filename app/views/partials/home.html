Your friendly Philly JavaScript conference.

Saturday, July 26th, 2014 at Impact Hub Philadelphia

The plan

We have lots of options for fun; if you register for a workshop, you have Intro, Node, and Angular to choose from.

In the afternoon, there will be open spaces sessions (a la BarCamp) and open hack time to work on Code for Philly projects, meet up with Girl Develop It Open Source Fellows, or work on practicing skills you learned if you chose to attend a workshop.

Finally, in the evening, we'll round it off with a good old fashioned social, thoroughly enjoying ImpactHub's deck.

More details coming soon! Meanwhile, if you'd like to help, contact the organizers through Meetup.
